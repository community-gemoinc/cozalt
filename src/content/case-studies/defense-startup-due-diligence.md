## Challenge

Apex Ventures was evaluating a significant Series A investment in a defense technology startup specializing in autonomous systems. They needed comprehensive due diligence to understand the technology viability, market positioning, regulatory landscape, and team capabilities before committing $15M.

## Our Approach

Cozalt deployed a multi-faceted due diligence process:

- **Technology Assessment**: Engaged subject matter experts to evaluate the core technology claims and competitive differentiation
- **Market Analysis**: Analyzed the addressable market within defense autonomous systems
- **Regulatory Review**: Mapped ITAR compliance requirements and export control considerations
- **Team Evaluation**: Conducted background checks and capability assessments of key personnel
- **Financial Modeling**: Validated revenue projections against realistic government procurement timelines

## Results

Our comprehensive report identified 12 key risk factors that needed mitigation, along with a clear path forward. Apex Ventures proceeded with the investment armed with full visibility into challenges and opportunities. The investment has since performed well, with the portfolio company securing two major DoD contracts within 18 months.
