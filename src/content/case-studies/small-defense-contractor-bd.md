## Challenge

Tactical Systems Inc., a small defense contractor with 25 employees, had excellent technical capabilities but lacked dedicated business development resources. They were missing opportunities because they couldn't effectively identify, track, and pursue government contracts while maintaining their technical delivery commitments.

## Our Approach

Cozalt provided outsourced business development services:

- **Opportunity Identification**: Implemented systematic monitoring of SAM.gov, GovWin, and agency forecasts relevant to the client's capabilities
- **Capture Management**: Developed and executed capture strategies for priority opportunities
- **Proposal Support**: Provided proposal management and writing support for competitive submissions
- **Relationship Building**: Facilitated introductions to prime contractors seeking small business partners
- **Process Development**: Built repeatable BD processes the client could eventually bring in-house

## Results

Over an 18-month engagement, Tactical Systems won four new contracts worth a combined $8.5M. Their opportunity pipeline grew from nearly zero to over $20M in tracked opportunities. Win rate improved from sporadic success to a consistent 35%. The company has since hired a full-time BD professional using the processes we developed as their foundation.
