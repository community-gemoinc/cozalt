## Challenge

Strategic Minerals Corp, a U.S.-based critical minerals company, sought to expand operations into Europe to capitalize on the EU's growing demand for domestically-sourced critical minerals. They faced challenges understanding regulatory requirements, identifying partners, and navigating the complex geopolitical landscape.

## Our Approach

Cozalt developed a phased expansion strategy:

- **Market Prioritization**: Analyzed EU member states to identify the three most promising markets based on regulatory environment, demand, and strategic fit
- **Regulatory Mapping**: Created comprehensive guides for mining permits, environmental compliance, and EU critical minerals regulations
- **Partner Identification**: Leveraged our network to connect the client with potential joint venture partners, offtake buyers, and government contacts
- **Entry Strategy**: Developed market-specific entry strategies balancing speed with risk mitigation

## Results

Within 12 months, Strategic Minerals Corp successfully established operations in Germany, Poland, and Finland. They secured five strategic partnerships including two government-backed initiatives and saw a 40% increase in revenue from European operations. The company is now positioned as a key supplier in the EU critical minerals supply chain.
